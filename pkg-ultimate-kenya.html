The Journey

Five Parks, One Unforgettable Narrative

Ten days is the minimum to genuinely understand Kenya. The Ultimate Kenya itinerary refuses to rush — instead it moves with the confidence of a journey that knows exactly what it is delivering. Five distinct ecosystems, each with their own character, their own cast of wildlife, their own quality of light.

From the Maasai Mara's open drama to the intimate riverine forests of Samburu; from Amboseli's Kilimanjaro panoramas to Lake Nakuru's flamingo spectacle and the red-earth wilderness of Tsavo — this is the complete Kenyan story, told without compromise.

Day by Day

Your Itinerary

Day 01

Nairobi → Maasai Mara

Wilson Airport · Mara airstrip

Charter flight to the Mara. Afternoon game drive. Luxury tented camp with private plunge pool. First sundowner on the plains.

  • Charter flight
  • Afternoon drive
  • Welcome dinner
Day 02

Maasai Mara Full Day

Maasai Mara National Reserve

Dawn to dusk in the Mara. Big cat tracking at sunrise. Bush breakfast in the field. Private conservancy afternoon. Maasai village at golden hour.

  • Dawn cat drive
  • Bush breakfast
  • Private conservancy
  • Maasai village
Day 03

Mara River & Balloon Dawn

Mara River Crossing Zone

Pre-dawn balloon flight over the Mara ecosystem. Champagne bush breakfast follows. Afternoon Mara River drive — crocodile and hippo spectacle.

  • Balloon safari
  • Champagne breakfast
  • River drive
  • Night drive
Day 04

Mara → Amboseli

Southern Kenya · Kilimanjaro Region

Charter south to Amboseli. Kilimanjaro reveals itself above the treeline as you descend. Afternoon elephant herds. Observation Hill at sunset.

  • Charter flight
  • Elephant drives
  • Kilimanjaro views
  • Observation Hill
Day 05

Full Amboseli Day

Amboseli National Park

The Amboseli elephants are among Africa's most studied. Bull elephants with magnificent tusks move across open plains against Kilimanjaro's changing light. Swamp walk with armed ranger. Cheetah and lion sightings on open flats.

  • Elephant drives
  • Swamp guided walk
  • Cheetah tracking
  • Night drive
Day 06

Amboseli → Lake Nakuru

Rift Valley · Nakuru National Park

Charter north to Lake Nakuru, the "Jewel of the Rift Valley." Rhino tracking — both black and white rhino inhabit the park. Flamingo spectacle at the lakeshore. Baboon Cliff viewpoints.

  • Charter flight
  • Rhino tracking
  • Flamingo lake drive
  • Baboon Cliff sunset
Day 07

Lake Nakuru → Samburu

Northern Kenya · Ewaso Ng'iro

Charter north to Samburu — the wild, sun-scorched north. Landscape and wildlife transform completely. Grevy's zebra, reticulated giraffe, gerenuk browsing on two legs. Riverside camp with hippo at dusk.

  • Charter north
  • Afternoon Samburu drive
  • River cocktails
  • Evening hippo watch
Day 08

Full Samburu Day

Samburu National Reserve

The "Samburu Five" endemic species are the focus: Grevy's zebra, reticulated giraffe, Somali ostrich, Beisa oryx, and gerenuk. Professional photography guide joins for the full day with custom camera rigs on the vehicle.

  • Photography guide
  • Endemic species drive
  • Cultural village visit
  • Night drive
Day 09

Samburu → Tsavo

Tsavo East · Red Earth Wilderness

Charter to Tsavo East — Kenya's largest national park and home to the famous "red elephants," coated in iron-rich volcanic dust. Vast herds, ancient lava flows, and Mzima Springs underground river system.

  • Charter to Tsavo
  • Red elephant drives
  • Mzima Springs walk
  • Sundowner at lava hill
Day 10

Tsavo → Nairobi · Farewell

Tsavo · Wilson Airport · Departure

Final dawn drive in Tsavo's extraordinary red landscape. Private breakfast at camp. Charter to Nairobi. Your Filmax Jambo team arranges a bespoke farewell dinner at a celebrated Nairobi restaurant if your schedule permits.

  • Final dawn drive
  • Charter to Nairobi
  • Farewell dinner option
  • Departure transfer
