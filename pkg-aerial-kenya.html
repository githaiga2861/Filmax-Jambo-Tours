The Journey

Kenya From the Sky Down

Every transfer by private aircraft. Every lodge at the absolute pinnacle of luxury. Every ecosystem viewed first from altitude, then experienced at ground level with a private guide and vehicle. Aerial Kenya is the definitive expression of what is possible in this country.

Four distinct ecosystems in eight days — the vast Mara plains, the elephant kingdom of Amboseli with its Kilimanjaro views at dawn, the wild north of Samburu, and the Indian Ocean finale at Diani. A personal photographer accompanies you for two days. The result is not just a safari — it is a portrait of Kenya assembled over eight extraordinary days.

Arrival & Logistics

Getting to Nairobi

Your international journey ends at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (NBO) in Nairobi. From there, Filmax Jambo takes complete charge. A dedicated transfer vehicle meets you at arrivals and delivers you to your pre-departure hotel or directly to Wilson Airport for same-day domestic charters.

🛬 Your Gateway
Jomo Kenyatta International Airport

IATA code: NBO. Located 15 km from Nairobi CBD, 18 km from Wilson Airport. Terminal 1A handles most international arrivals. Filmax Jambo arranges all airport transfers in air-conditioned private vehicles.

✈️ Domestic Hub
Wilson Airport — Your Safari Departure Point

All private charter flights depart from Wilson Airport (WIL), Nairobi — Kenya's primary light aviation hub, 8 km south of the CBD. Transfers from JKIA or city hotels are arranged by your concierge. Relaxed security, private lounges available.

🏨 Pre-Safari Option
Nairobi Pre-Safari Night

We recommend arriving the night before your safari begins. Nairobi's luxury hotel options include the Hemingways Nairobi, The Norfolk, and Giraffe Manor. We can arrange accommodation and a Nairobi city programme on request.

🌍 End Point
Departing from Mombasa

The itinerary concludes at Moi International Airport (MBA), Mombasa, with connections to Nairobi and direct international routes. Alternatively, a private charter can return you directly to Nairobi from Diani on request.

Entry Requirements

Visas, Health & Practicalities

Kenya VisaMost nationalities require an e-visa. Apply at evisa.go.ke. Currently USD 50/person. 90-day single entry. Apply at least 2 weeks before travel.
Yellow FeverCertificate required if arriving from or transiting a yellow fever endemic country. Recommended regardless.
Malaria ProphylaxisRequired for all safari destinations. Consult your GP 6–8 weeks before travel. Malarone or Doxycycline most commonly prescribed.
AltitudeNairobi sits at 1,795m. Some guests feel mild effects on day 1. All safari destinations are at lower elevation.
CurrencyUSD widely accepted. Kenyan Shilling (KES) for local purchases. ATMs available at JKIA and Nairobi city. No need to carry cash after check-in.
Time ZoneEast Africa Time (EAT) — UTC +3. No daylight saving. London is 3 hrs behind, New York 8 hrs behind.
Electricity240V / 50Hz. British-style Type G plugs (3-pin square). Universal adapters provided at all lodges.
CommunicationsWiFi available at all lodges (signal varies by location). Safaricom SIM recommended for data. Emergency satellite communication arranged by lodge for remote areas.
