The Journey

Kenya From the Sky Down

Every transfer by private aircraft. Every lodge at the absolute pinnacle of luxury. Every ecosystem viewed first from altitude, then experienced at ground level with a private guide and vehicle. Aerial Kenya is the definitive expression of what is possible in this country.

Four distinct ecosystems in eight days — the vast Mara plains, the elephant kingdom of Amboseli with its Kilimanjaro views at dawn, the wild north of Samburu, and the Indian Ocean finale at Diani. A personal photographer accompanies you for two days. The result is not just a safari — it is a portrait of Kenya assembled over eight extraordinary days.
